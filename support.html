Frequently Asked Questions

How do I unlock premium stories?

Tap the locked story card in the Story Library, then tap the purchase button. Premium content is a one-time purchase that unlocks all premium stories permanently.

How do I restore my purchases?

If you've previously purchased premium content and need to restore it (e.g., after reinstalling the app or switching devices), go to Settings and tap "Restore Purchases". Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.

The app is not playing videos. What should I do?

Please ensure you have a stable internet connection, as stories are streamed. Try closing and reopening the app. If the issue persists, please contact us.

Is StoryBoo safe for children?

Yes! StoryBoo is designed specifically for young children. All content is age-appropriate, and advertisements shown are child-directed and comply with COPPA regulations. We do not collect personal information from children.

How do I remove ads?

Ads are removed automatically when you purchase premium content. Once unlocked, premium stories play without any advertisements.

Which devices are supported?

StoryBoo supports all iPhones and iPads running iOS 17.0 or later. The app is optimized for landscape orientation.

I need a refund. What should I do?

Refunds for in-app purchases are handled by Apple. You can request a refund through Apple's Report a Problem page or by contacting Apple Support.
